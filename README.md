## Customer Shopping Behavior Analysis | Python, PostgreSQL, Power BI

1. Cleaned and transformed 3,900 retail transaction records in Python using pandas, handling missing review ratings through category-wise median imputation and engineering features like age groups and purchase frequency for downstream analysis. 
2. Performed structured SQL analysis in PostgreSQL across 10 business queries covering revenue by gender, customer segmentation, discount dependency, and subscription behavior to extract actionable insights from transactional data.
3. Identified that 73% of customers were non-subscribers despite comparable average spend, and flagged 5 discount-dependent products with 47-50% discount rates, recommending targeted subscription campaigns and a revised discount policy to protect margins.

### Recommendations from analysis
1. Introducing Loyalty Programs to repeat buyers to increase retention.
2. Highlighting top-rated and best selling products in market campaigns will position the product and promoting exclusive benefits for subscribers will also increase revenue.
